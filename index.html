Flagship project

TableArc

A local-first AI tabletop RPG engine built around canonical state, rules-aware procedure, persistent worlds, provider flexibility, and inspectable runtime behavior.

The AI imagines. The engine remembers, adjudicates, maps, resolves, and proves.

TableArc is not simply a fantasy chatbot. It connects natural-language play to structured adventure state, character authority, maps, combat, rules, continuity, saves, and debugging.

How the work is approached

Make complexity legible.

The common thread across GreenKappa Labs projects is a preference for systems that are expressive on the surface and explicit underneath.

01

Clarify the real problem

Separate the user’s goal from the accumulated interface, process, or implementation noise.

02

Give each system an owner

Use clear contracts, structured state, and visible boundaries instead of hidden accidental behavior.

03

Prove the result

Validate the path that matters through real use, diagnostics, accessibility, and durable outputs.
