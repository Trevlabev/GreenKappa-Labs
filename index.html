Flagship project

TableArc

An active GreenKappa Labs project exploring how AI-assisted tabletop play can become structured, state-aware, local-first, and still creatively alive.

A local-first AI tabletop RPG engine

TableArc combines natural-language play with structured runtime state, local language-model integration, rules-aware workflows, tactical maps, combat context, continuity tools, and debug transparency.

The goal is not merely a chatbot that tells fantasy stories. It is a playable tabletop engine where narration, character authority, scene structure, mechanics, maps, consequences, and system state work together.
